About Me

My name is Kevin Gallegos, a graduate from UC Santa Cruz, with a degree in Computer Science with a focus in Game Design. Since the day I could organize my thoughts I always liked the idea of being able to create stuff. I took inspiration by the world and enviroment around me and was curious on how stuff worked. I remember myself creating toys out of paper to play with and drawing comics as a medium to share my thoughts and feelings. Overtime this curiosity, the idea of creating and sharing led me to the world of computers and from there I found a medium that I could express myself in. Coming up to college I experimented with modding games and personalizing my computer, obtaining a fond intrest in everything from games to building computers. I learned invaluable skills in communication and leadership working at Summer Camps. All of these intrests and skills culminating together to allow me to pursue my intrest in sharing with the world something that I can make and be interacted with using the medium of programmable media, Games.
